### 2. Description of Services

(1) Music Logger is an app for the *automated recognition and logging of music* played at events, parties, or comparable occasions.

(2) The App captures the following information:
* Recognized song title
* Recognized artist (author)
* Time of playback
* Location or event reference (if provided by user)

(3) *The following are NOT captured or stored:*
* Conversations or voice recordings
* Voices of individuals
* Other audio content outside of music
* Personal data of guests or third parties

(4) The App serves specifically for the documentation of played music and the avoidance of song duplications.

(5) Music recognition is performed automatically via external music recognition interfaces, primarily utilizing **Shazam (Apple)** technology.

(6) The average recognition accuracy of the App is *approx. 80%*. A complete or error-free recognition of all played songs is technically not possible.

(7) The functionality of the App is particularly restricted or excluded if:
* unreleased songs or songs not recorded in databases are played,
* songs are heavily altered in their speed, pitch, or structure,
* there is no stable internet connection,
* the audio quality at the recording location is insufficient.
