    General Terms and Conditions (GTC)

    1. Scope of Application

    These General Terms and Conditions (GTC) govern the use of the app "Music Logger" as well as the associated web app (hereinafter collectively referred to as "App") by consumers within the meaning of § 13 BGB (German Civil Code).

    The provider of the App is:

    Dj Chami & Chantre Productions UG Germanusstrasse 30 52080 Aachen Germany VAT ID: DE341535251 Email: chami@dancefusionaachen.de

    Deviating terms and conditions of the user shall not apply.

    2. Description of Services

    (1) Music Logger is an app for the automated recognition and logging of music played at events, parties, or comparable occasions.

    (2) The App captures exclusively the following information:

    • Recognized song title
    • Recognized artist (author)
    • Time of playback
    • Location of usage (event or location reference)

    (3) The following are NOT captured or stored:

    • Conversations or voice recordings
    • Voices of individuals
    • Other audio content outside of music
    • Personal data of guests or third parties

    (4) The App serves specifically for the documentation of played music, the analysis of playlists, and the avoidance of song duplications.

    (5) Music recognition is performed automatically via external music recognition interfaces (APIs), specifically utilizing Shazam technology.

    (6) The average recognition accuracy of the App is approx. 80%. A complete or error-free recognition of all played songs is technically not possible.

    (7) The functionality of the App is particularly restricted or excluded if:

    • unreleased songs or songs not recorded in databases are played,
    • songs are heavily altered in their speed, pitch, or structure,
    • there is no internet connection or an unstable internet connection,
    • the audio quality at the recording location is insufficient.

    (8) There is no claim to successful music recognition in every individual case.

    3. Activation and Consent

    (1) Music recognition occurs exclusively following active usage by the user. Recording does not take place automatically and not without the user's knowledge.

    (2) By using the App, the user confirms that they are authorized to perform music recognition at the respective location and that no third-party rights are being infringed.

    (1) Use of the App requires the creation of a user account (Login).

    (2) The user is obligated to provide truthful information during registration and to keep their access data confidential.

    (3) The provider is entitled to block or delete user accounts in the event of violations of these GTC.

    (1) Music Logger is offered in a free basic version as well as in paid subscription models.

    (2) The respective scope of functions depends on the selected tariff.

    (3) Prices, terms, and cancellation conditions of the subscriptions are displayed transparently prior to the conclusion of the contract and form part of the contract.

    6. Display and Export of Logs

    (1) Users can view their recorded music logs:

    • within the App and
    • export them in suitable file formats.

    (2) The responsibility for the further use, transfer, or publication of the exported data lies exclusively with the user.

    7. User Obligations

    (1) The App must not be used:

    (2) The user ensures that the use of the App does not violate applicable laws, domiciliary rights (house rules), or contractual obligations.

    (1) No guarantee is assumed for the completeness, correctness, or recognition accuracy of the music recognition.

    (2) The provider is not liable for:

    • incorrect or incomplete song recognitions,
    • technical failures or restrictions,

    (4) The provider is not liable for restrictions resulting from the use of external music recognition services (e.g., Shazam API), lack of internet connection, or altered music parameters.

    (5) The App represents a supporting documentation tool and not a legally binding or evidentiary protocol.
